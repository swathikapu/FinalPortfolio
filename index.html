About

I am Swathi, a Computer Science Engineering graduate who is passionate about becoming a Computer Programmer. My zeal to learn programming started a few years ago when I started to realize how well it is simplifying a lot of tasks in our day to day life. The thought of solving world problems and simplifying day to day chores triggered the zeal in me to learn principles and techniques used in building websites, writing apps and developing games etc.

Recently I decided to start my career in IT and I enrolled into a self-funded IT training program with Just IT. I have updated myself with the latest technologies in developing web applications. I have worked on few individual projects with my main focus in backend development using C#, MVC 5.0, ASP.NET, SQL Server, LINQ, HTML5, CSS3.
